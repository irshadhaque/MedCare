Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment by navigating to our booking section, selecting your preferred clinic or hospital, choosing a date and time, and filling out the required information.

Can I access my medical reports online?

Yes! Once your tests are completed, you will receive an email notification, and you can access your reports directly through our website in your account.

What should I do if I have trouble accessing my reports?

If you're experiencing any issues, please reach out to our customer support team through the contact page, and we’ll assist you promptly.

Are online consultations available?

Absolutely! We offer online consultations with qualified healthcare professionals. Simply choose the “Online Consultation” option and select a suitable time for your appointment.

Is there a fee for online consultations?

Yes, online consultations may have a fee, which will be displayed during the booking process. We strive to keep our rates competitive and transparent.

What types of clinics and hospitals can I book through your site?

We partner with various clinics and hospitals, including general practice, specialists, and diagnostic centers. You can browse our extensive list to find the right provider for your needs.

How do I change or cancel my appointment?

To change or cancel your appointment, go to your account dashboard, locate your upcoming appointments, and follow the prompts to make adjustments.

Can I book appointments for someone else?

Yes, you can book appointments on behalf of family members or friends. Just provide their details during the booking process.

Is my personal information safe with your website?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely in accordance with data protection regulations.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the contact form on our website, or by emailing us directly at [your email address]. We’re here to help!
